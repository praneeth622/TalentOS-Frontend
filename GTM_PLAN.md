# TalentOS — GTM & Monetization Plan

Short go-to-market and revenue strategy for TalentOS.

---

## 1. Target Company Size & HR Personas

| Segment | Company size | Persona | Primary need |
|--------|--------------|---------|----------------|
| **SMB** | 10–100 employees | HR Manager / Office Admin | One-person HR: hiring, tasks, basic productivity view. Low touch, self-serve. |
| **Mid-market** | 100–500 employees | HR Lead / Talent Ops | Team productivity, skill gaps, AI insights. Willing to pay for analytics and AI. |
| **Enterprise (pilot)** | 500–2,000 employees | Head of Talent / HR Tech Buyer | Scalable workforce intelligence, compliance, integrations. Pilot-first, then expand. |

**Ideal customer profile (ICP):** Tech-enabled SMBs and mid-market companies (SaaS, services, product) with 20–200 employees, already using tools like Slack/Notion and open to AI-augmented HR.

---

## 2. Three-Month Roadmap to Onboard First 50–100 Companies

| Month | Focus | Target outcome |
|-------|--------|-----------------|
| **Month 1 — Launch & distribution** | Product hunt, LinkedIn, 2–3 HR communities (e.g. SHRM India, HR Tech Facebook groups). Free tier + 14-day trial. | 200 sign-ups, 30 activated orgs (created at least 1 employee + 1 task). |
| **Month 2 — Sales & success** | Outbound to 500 ICPs (email + LinkedIn). 2 webinars: “AI for small HR teams” and “Productivity scoring in 15 min.” Case study from 3 early customers. | 60 activated orgs, 15 paying (see revenue streams below). First 50 companies in pipeline. |
| **Month 3 — Scale & retain** | Referral program (1 month free for referrer + referee). Integrations page + 1 integration (e.g. Slack or Google Workspace). NPS survey and fix top churn reasons. | 100+ activated orgs, 30–40 paying. First 50–100 companies achieved. |

**Activation definition:** Org registered, added ≥1 employee, created ≥1 task. **Paying:** On a paid plan (see revenue streams).

---

## 3. ₹5,000 Experimental Marketing Plan

| Channel | Budget (₹) | Action |
|---------|------------|--------|
| **LinkedIn** | 2,000 | Boost 2 posts (product launch + one “AI skill gap” use case) to HR/Talent audience in India. |
| **Google / Meta** | 1,500 | Small search/social test: keywords “HR software for small business”, “employee productivity tool”. Landing page with clear CTA and demo link. |
| **Community & content** | 1,000 | 2–3 micro-influencer/HR blogger collabs (post + story). One short demo video (2–3 min) for YouTube and LinkedIn. |
| **Events / swag** | 500 | Digital one-pager + QR to sign-up. Optional: small giveaway (e.g. 3 months free) for first 20 sign-ups from a specific community or webinar. |

**Success metrics:** Cost per sign-up (CPS), cost per activated org (CPA). Aim: CPA &lt; ₹200 in experiment phase.

---

## 4. Revenue Streams (Minimum 2)

| Stream | Model | Description |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| **1. HR SaaS subscription** | Monthly/annual per-seat or per-org tier | **Free:** Up to 5 employees, 1 org admin. **Starter (₹999/mo):** Up to 25 employees, basic analytics. **Growth (₹2,499/mo):** Up to 100 employees, AI insights, skill gap, smart assign. **Scale (custom):** 100+ employees, SSO, priority support. |
| **2. Analytics & reporting modules** | Add-on or higher tier | **Insights pack:** Exportable reports (productivity by team/department, completion trends). One-time or ₹499/mo add-on. **Custom dashboards:** Pre-built templates (e.g. “Recruiter view”, “Manager view”) as part of Growth/Scale. |
| **3. AI insights add-ons** | Add-on usage or tier | **AI Copilot / daily insight:** Included in Growth+. **Extra AI usage:** Beyond X queries/month, pay-per-use or top-up packs (e.g. ₹299 for 50 extra AI calls). **Resume parsing:** Per-parse or bulk pack (e.g. 50 parses/month in Growth). |

**Primary revenue:** (1) SaaS subscription. **Secondary:** (2) Analytics modules and (3) AI add-ons. All three satisfy “minimum 2 revenue streams” with clear HR SaaS + add-ons (analytics + AI).

---

## 5. Summary

- **Who:** SMB & mid-market (10–500 employees); persona: HR Manager / Talent Ops.
- **First 50–100 companies:** 3-month plan via product launch, community, outbound, webinars, referral, and one integration.
- **₹5,000 experiment:** LinkedIn, small paid search/social, content/collabs, one demo video, light events.
- **Revenue:** (1) SaaS tiers, (2) Analytics/reporting add-ons, (3) AI usage add-ons.
